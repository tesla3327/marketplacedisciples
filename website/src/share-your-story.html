Do I qualify for an interview?

I interview all sorts of Christian business owners and entrepreneurs (freelancers, too!)

If you love the Lord and run a business, you're qualified. Whether you have 2 employees or 200 (or even 2000), or you're on your own, it doesn't matter. If you've been doing this for 40 years, or for a few months, you are qualified.

Even if you don't run a business, but have a unique professional experience (such as being in upper management), you qualify.

Marketplace Disciples becomes a lot more interesting the more diverse the interviews are, so I'd love to have you on board!

How much work do I have to put in?

You can put in as little or as much time into this as you want. I aim to keep my process flexible and to make you as comfortable as possible.

How long does it take?

The whole process can last several weeks from beginning to end, largely dependent on how many other interviews I have going on at the time, and how busy I am otherwise (I do this in my spare time, after all).

However, the process is based around email, so you are never actually waiting around. It's just a matter of how quickly we are responding to each other's emails.

What is the process?

NOTE: I will NEVER publish anything that you are not comfortable with.

First, I will send you some questions by email, and you can take your time answering them. Write as little or as much as you want, there is no specific length I am looking for.

Based on these answers I will send you back some follow up questions. These questions allow me to dive a little deeper.

Once I receive the last set of answers I will edit the interview and send it back to you for review.

Finally, when you have given me the thumbs up on the interview I will publish it on the site!

Contact me

Send me an email and let me know you would like to do an interview.

You can contact me at: michael@marketplacedisciples.com
