Marketplace Disciples

Interviews with Christian leaders in business

Hi, my name is Michael.

For the past several months I have been working on this new project — interviewing Christians who are in the business world. These are people who work for themselves or own small businesses. But most importantly, they love the Lord.

I talk to them about what they do, and also dig deeper to understand how their faith motivates them and radically changes how they do what they do.

I started these interviews a few months ago because I want to encourage Christians who own their own business or work for themselves. How faith and business can come together to serve God's kingdom is a topic that isn't often talked about, and I want to push that conversation forward.

Hopefully through these interviews you will find ideas, inspiration, and encouragement.

I am still working on getting the initial interviews and website together, but if you're excited about this please sign up below and I will let you know when it launches!
